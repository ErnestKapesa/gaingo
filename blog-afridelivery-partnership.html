Afridelivery & Gaingo EV: Potential Partnership for Greener Deliveries in Zambia

February 7, 2026
5 min read
Back to Blog

Afridelivery, the trailblazers in biking delivery services, are having discussions on joining forces with Gaingo EV, Zambia's pioneering electric bike innovators. The potential of this collaboration promises greener deliveries, massive emission cuts, and plenty of new jobs for bike riders across the country.

A Vision for Clean Mobility in Africa

Gaingo, a cutting-edge new-energy mobility tech company and subsidiary of Smartcity Zambia, envisions advancing clean, low-carbon transportation throughout Africa. Their mission focuses on empowering transport entrepreneurs with high-quality electric motorcycles, rentals, and a super-fast battery-swapping network—under 5 minutes per swap—to slash fuel costs and build profitable, sustainable businesses.

Revolutionizing Last-Mile Logistics

Afridelivery's biking expertise combined with Gaingo's EV prowess will revolutionize last-mile logistics in Lusaka and beyond. This potential partnership represents a significant step forward in transforming how goods and services are delivered across Zambia's urban centers.

What This Means for Riders and Businesses

  • Reliable E-Bikes: Access to high-quality electric motorcycles designed for delivery services
  • Massive Fuel Savings: Businesses save significantly on operational costs by switching from petrol to electric
  • Super-Fast Battery Swapping: Under 5 minutes per swap means minimal downtime and maximum earnings
  • Green Jobs: More employment opportunities in Zambia's growing green economy
  • Emission Cuts: Contributing to cleaner air and a healthier environment across Zambian cities

Empowering Transport Entrepreneurs

At the heart of this potential collaboration is a shared commitment to empowering transport entrepreneurs. Gaingo's innovative approach provides riders with the tools they need to build profitable, sustainable businesses while Afridelivery's extensive delivery network offers the platform to put those tools to work.

The combination of reliable electric motorcycles, affordable rental options, and a rapidly expanding battery-swapping network creates an ecosystem where delivery riders can thrive without the burden of high fuel costs and expensive vehicle maintenance.

Building a Sustainable Future

As Zambia moves toward a sustainable future, partnerships like this one between Afridelivery and Gaingo EV are essential. They demonstrate that environmental responsibility and business profitability can go hand in hand, creating opportunities for growth while protecting our planet.

The green economy isn't just about reducing emissions—it's about creating new opportunities, new jobs, and new ways of doing business that benefit everyone. From delivery riders earning more to businesses saving on costs, to communities enjoying cleaner air, the benefits ripple across society.

The Road Ahead

While discussions are ongoing, the potential impact of this collaboration is already generating excitement across Zambia's logistics and transportation sectors. The combination of Afridelivery's proven delivery expertise and Gaingo's cutting-edge electric mobility technology positions both companies to lead the transformation of urban logistics in Zambia and beyond.

Join the Electric Revolution

Whether you're a delivery rider looking for better opportunities, a business seeking to reduce costs, or simply someone who cares about Zambia's sustainable future, this potential partnership represents the kind of innovation that moves us all forward.

Stay tuned for updates as these discussions progress!
