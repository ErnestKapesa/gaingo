Gaingo Partners with Afridelivery: Revolutionizing Last-Mile Delivery in Zambia

February 7, 2026
5 min read
Back to Blog

Gaingo, Zambia's leading electric motorbike battery swapping company, is proud to announce a strategic partnership with Afridelivery, one of Africa's fastest-growing last-mile delivery platforms. This collaboration marks a significant milestone in transforming urban logistics across Zambia through sustainable, cost-effective electric mobility solutions.

A Partnership Built on Shared Vision

Both Gaingo and Afridelivery share a common goal: to revolutionize transportation and logistics in Africa through innovative, sustainable solutions. While Afridelivery has been connecting businesses with reliable delivery services across Zambia, Gaingo has been pioneering electric mobility through its revolutionary battery swapping technology.

Together, we're creating a powerful synergy that addresses the biggest challenges facing delivery businesses today: high fuel costs, vehicle maintenance expenses, and environmental impact.

What This Partnership Means for Delivery Businesses

This strategic collaboration brings together Afridelivery's extensive logistics network with Gaingo's cutting-edge electric motorbike infrastructure. Here's what delivery businesses can expect:

Key Benefits of the Partnership

  • Up to 70% Cost Savings: Electric motorbikes dramatically reduce operational costs compared to petrol bikes
  • Zero Downtime: Battery swapping in under 2 minutes means riders stay on the road earning
  • Expanded Coverage: Growing network of swap stations across Lusaka and beyond
  • Reliable Fleet: Access to well-maintained electric motorbikes with guaranteed uptime
  • Environmental Impact: Zero emissions contributing to cleaner air in Zambian cities

Transforming Last-Mile Delivery

Last-mile delivery is the most expensive and challenging part of the logistics chain. Traditional petrol motorbikes, while popular, come with significant operational costs and environmental concerns. This partnership addresses these challenges head-on.

Afridelivery riders using Gaingo's electric motorbikes will benefit from:

  • Instant Battery Swapping: No more waiting hours for charging - swap and go in under 2 minutes
  • Always Charged: Every swap provides 100% battery charge for maximum range
  • Pay-As-You-Go: Flexible pricing that scales with your business needs
  • Maintenance-Free: Gaingo handles all battery maintenance and management
  • Growing Network: Expanding swap stations ensure coverage wherever you deliver

Real Impact on Delivery Economics

The numbers speak for themselves. A typical delivery rider using a petrol motorbike spends approximately K150-200 per day on fuel. With Gaingo's electric motorbikes, that cost drops to K45-60 per day - a savings of over K100 daily, or K3,000+ per month.

For delivery businesses operating fleets of 10, 50, or 100+ riders, these savings multiply dramatically. The partnership enables Afridelivery to offer more competitive rates to businesses while riders take home more earnings.

Environmental Leadership

Beyond economics, this partnership represents a commitment to environmental sustainability. Every electric motorbike removes harmful emissions from Zambia's cities, contributing to cleaner air and a healthier environment for all citizens.

With Afridelivery's extensive delivery network now powered by Gaingo's electric fleet, we're making a measurable impact on urban air quality while proving that sustainable business practices are also the most profitable.

What's Next

The partnership is already operational, with Afridelivery riders in Lusaka now using Gaingo's electric motorbikes and battery swapping stations. Over the coming months, we'll be expanding to additional cities including Kitwe, Ndola, and Livingstone.

We're also working on integrated technology solutions that will make fleet management even more efficient, including real-time battery monitoring, optimized swap station routing, and predictive maintenance alerts.

Join the Electric Delivery Revolution

Whether you're a delivery business looking to reduce costs, a rider wanting to maximize earnings, or a company seeking sustainable logistics solutions, the Gaingo-Afridelivery partnership offers the perfect solution.

Contact us today to learn how you can benefit from this groundbreaking partnership.
