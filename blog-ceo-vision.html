Kevin Lu's Vision: Transforming Africa Through Clean Energy

January 14, 2025
8 min read
Back to Blog

Kevin Lu

Chief Executive Officer, Gaingo

Visionary entrepreneur with over 10 years of experience in sustainable energy and transportation. Leading Gaingo's mission to revolutionize electric mobility across Africa.

When Kevin Lu founded Gaingo, he didn't just see a business opportunity—he saw a chance to transform millions of lives across Africa. His vision extends far beyond battery swapping stations and electric motorbikes. It's about creating lasting social impact, empowering communities, and building a sustainable future for the continent.

A Vision Born from Purpose

"I came to Zambia and saw incredible potential," Kevin reflects. "I saw hardworking delivery riders spending half their income on fuel. I saw taxi operators struggling with unpredictable costs. I saw cities choked with pollution. But I also saw opportunity—opportunity to create real change."

That opportunity became Gaingo, Zambia's first and leading electric motorbike battery swapping company. But for Kevin, Gaingo represents something much bigger than a business venture.

Our mission isn't just to sell electric motorbikes. It's to empower every Zambian rider with affordable, reliable, and sustainable transportation. It's to create jobs, build infrastructure, and prove that Africa can lead the global clean energy revolution.

— Kevin Lu, CEO of Gaingo

Changing Lives, One Rider at a Time

Kevin's vision is already transforming lives across Zambia. Delivery riders who once spent K2,000-K3,000 monthly on fuel now save up to 70% with Gaingo's battery swapping. That's not just savings—it's life-changing money that goes toward education, healthcare, and building better futures for families.

Real Impact Stories

Kevin shares the story of Joseph, a delivery rider in Lusaka: "Joseph told me he was able to send his daughter to a better school because of the money he saves with Gaingo. That's what drives me. That's why we do this."

These aren't isolated cases. Across Zambia, Gaingo riders are:

  • Earning more income with lower operating costs
  • Working longer hours without fuel stops or charging waits
  • Building more stable businesses with predictable expenses
  • Investing savings in their families and communities
  • Contributing to cleaner air in their neighborhoods

Reshaping Businesses Across Zambia

Kevin's vision extends to transforming how businesses operate in Zambia. Delivery companies, logistics firms, and taxi services are discovering that electric mobility isn't just environmentally responsible—it's economically smart.

Business Transformation

Companies partnering with Gaingo report:

  • 40-70% reduction in fleet operating costs
  • Increased productivity with no downtime for charging
  • Enhanced brand reputation through sustainability
  • Improved employee satisfaction with reliable vehicles
  • Competitive advantage in the market

"We're not just providing a service," Kevin explains. "We're enabling businesses to thrive. When a delivery company switches to Gaingo, they don't just save money—they become more competitive, more efficient, and more sustainable."

Building Infrastructure for Africa's Future

Kevin's vision includes creating robust infrastructure that will serve Zambia and eventually all of Africa. Gaingo's battery swapping stations are more than charging points—they're hubs of economic activity creating local employment and skills development.

The Infrastructure Vision

  • Expanding Network: Swapping stations in every major city and town
  • Job Creation: Thousands of jobs in station operations, maintenance, and support
  • Skills Development: Training Zambians in electric vehicle technology
  • Local Manufacturing: Plans to manufacture batteries and components in Africa
  • Regional Expansion: Bringing the model to other African countries

Environmental Stewardship

"Clean air isn't a luxury—it's a right," Kevin emphasizes. "Every child in Lusaka, Kitwe, and Ndola deserves to breathe clean air. Every community deserves a healthy environment."

Under Kevin's leadership, Gaingo is contributing to:

  • Significant reduction in urban air pollution
  • Lower carbon emissions across Zambia's transportation sector
  • Demonstration that economic growth and environmental protection go hand-in-hand
  • Setting standards for sustainable business practices in Africa

The Africa Vision

Kevin's ambitions extend beyond Zambia. He sees Gaingo as a model for all of Africa—proof that African companies can lead in clean technology and sustainable development.

"Africa doesn't need to follow the same polluting path that other continents took," Kevin states. "We can leapfrog directly to clean energy. We can build transportation systems that are sustainable from day one. Zambia is showing the way."

Continental Expansion Plans

Kevin is already laying groundwork for regional expansion:

  • Partnerships with governments across Southern Africa
  • Technology transfer to enable local manufacturing
  • Training programs to develop African talent in EV technology
  • Investment in renewable energy to power the network
  • Collaboration with African universities on research and development

Empowering the Next Generation

Kevin is passionate about creating opportunities for young Zambians. Gaingo actively recruits and trains young people in electric vehicle technology, business operations, and customer service.

"These young people aren't just employees—they're the future leaders of Africa's clean energy revolution," Kevin says. "We're investing in their skills, their careers, and their futures."

A Message to Zambia

Kevin's message to Zambians is one of optimism and partnership: "Gaingo's success is Zambia's success. Every rider who switches to electric, every business that partners with us, every station we open—these are steps toward a better future for all Zambians."

He continues: "I believe in Zambia's potential. I believe in the ingenuity, hard work, and resilience of Zambian people. Together, we're not just building a company—we're building a movement. We're proving that sustainable development is possible, profitable, and powerful."

The future of African transportation is electric, sustainable, and empowering. Zambia is leading the way, and Gaingo is proud to be part of this transformation. This is just the beginning.

— Kevin Lu, CEO of Gaingo

Join the Movement

Kevin's vision is ambitious, but it's already becoming reality. Thousands of Zambians have joined the electric mobility revolution. Businesses are transforming their operations. Communities are breathing cleaner air. Lives are changing.

The question isn't whether electric mobility will transform Africa—it's whether you'll be part of making it happen.
