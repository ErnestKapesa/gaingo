The 4-Step Swapping Process

1

Locate a Swapping Station

Use the Gaingo mobile app to find the nearest battery swapping station. Our app shows real-time availability, so you always know where charged batteries are waiting for you.

2

Remove Your Depleted Battery

Simply unlock your battery compartment and slide out the depleted battery. Our smart system automatically registers your swap and prepares a fully charged battery.

3

Insert a Fully Charged Battery

Grab a 100% charged battery from the station and insert it into your motorbike. It clicks securely into place with our smart locking mechanism.

4

Ride Away - Total Time: Under 2 Minutes!

That's it! You're back on the road with a full charge and maximum range. Payment is automatic through your Gaingo account.

The Business Behind the Swap

Smart Battery Management

Every Gaingo battery is equipped with IoT sensors that monitor:

  • Battery health and performance
  • Charge level and capacity
  • Temperature and safety status
  • Usage patterns and optimization

Automated Station Operations

Our swapping stations use intelligent systems to:

  • Charge batteries optimally for longevity
  • Rotate battery inventory efficiently
  • Maintain ideal storage conditions
  • Ensure only fully charged, healthy batteries are available

Secure & Seamless Transactions

The entire swap process is tracked digitally. When you remove a battery, our system knows. When you insert a new one, payment is processed automatically. No cash, no cards, no hassle.
