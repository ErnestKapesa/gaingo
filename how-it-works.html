How Gaingo Battery Swapping Works

Revolutionary battery swapping in under 2 minutes. Simple, fast, and efficient.

1

Find Nearest Station

Use our mobile app or website to locate the nearest Gaingo battery swapping station. Real-time availability shows you which stations have charged batteries ready for you.

2

Remove Depleted Battery

Simply unlock your battery compartment and slide out the depleted battery. Our smart system automatically registers your swap and prepares a fully charged battery for you.

3

Insert Charged Battery

Grab a 100% charged battery from the station and insert it into your motorbike. It clicks securely into place, and you're ready to go!

4

Ride Away - Under 2 Minutes!

That's it! The entire process takes less than 2 minutes. Continue your journey with a fully charged battery and maximum range. Pay only for what you use through our flexible pricing plans.
