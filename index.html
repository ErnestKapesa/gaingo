How Battery Swapping Works

Simple, fast, and efficient - the future of electric mobility

1

Locate Station

Use our app to find the nearest Gaingo battery swapping station. Real-time availability ensures you always find a charged battery.

2

Remove Empty Battery

Simply unlock your battery compartment and remove the depleted battery. Our standardized design makes it effortless.

3

Insert Fresh Battery

Grab a fully charged battery from the station and insert it into your motorbike. It clicks into place securely.

4

Ride Away

You're ready to go! The entire process takes less than 60 seconds. Continue your journey with zero downtime.
